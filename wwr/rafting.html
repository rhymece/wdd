What could be better than a rafting trip to cool off on a hot summer day?

Accessible and safe, our activities will please everyone! Get out of the ordinary and come and surpass yourself with us. Located just a few minutes from downtown, we offer descents adapted to your abilities.

The family descent:

This descent has been specially designed to allow young children, and people less comfortable in the water to live the rafting experience at a lower intensity. Without compromising on fun and safety, you'll face class 1 and 2 waves. However, you will be able to admire the biggest waves of the Lachine Rapids! If your guide allows you and if you feel like it, you will have the option to end the experience with a short swim while letting yourself be lulled by small currents. Nothing better for an initiation and a great day with the family!

The adventurous descent:

Are you thirsty for adrenaline and have no problem getting splashed by waves? The Adventurer is the descent of choice! Enjoy an outdoor activity and discover the nature in a fun and refreshing way. An introduction for young and old to the pleasures of white water excursions!
